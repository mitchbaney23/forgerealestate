Simple, Transparent, and Effective

Our process is designed to get you from listing to sold with clarity and confidence.

Step 1: Submit Your Property Details

It all starts with our simple online form. Provide some basic information about your home, and our system gets to work. This initial step takes just a few minutes and gives us the foundation for your sale.

Step 2: Receive an Instant Automated Estimate

Using advanced algorithms and public data, our tool provides an instant estimate of your property's probable selling price. This Automated Valuation Model (AVM) is a powerful, data-driven starting point for understanding your home's position in the current market.

Step 3: Get an Expert-Prepared CMA

This is where human expertise makes the difference. Mitch will personally take your information and prepare a detailed Comparative Market Analysis (CMA), cross-referencing your property with the most up-to-date, private MLS data. This provides a much more refined and accurate price opinion for your listing strategy.

Step 4: Launch & Sell Seamlessly

Once we agree on a strategy, your property goes live with full service marketing. Your information is securely managed in our CRM, and we handle everything from showings and negotiations to contract management, guiding you all the way to a successful closing.
