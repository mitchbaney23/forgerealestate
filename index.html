Yes, absolutely. "Full service" means we handle every critical aspect of your home sale. This includes a professional market analysis (CMA), listing your home on the Triangle MLS and all major real estate websites (Zillow, Realtor.com, etc.), professional photography, expert negotiation of offers, and complete contract-to-close management. You get the full experience, just for a fairer price.

The instant estimate is an automated valuation model (AVM) that provides a preliminary price point based on public data and algorithms. It's a great starting point. The Comparative Market Analysis (CMA), which Mitch prepares personally, is a much more detailed and accurate pricing opinion. It involves an expert analysis of the MLS, considering the unique features of your home, its condition, and the very latest market trends that only a licensed professional can access.

Yes. To ensure your property gets maximum exposure to all potential buyers, it's standard practice in North Carolina to offer a commission to the agent who brings the buyer (typically 2.5-3%). Our model saves you money on the *listing side* of the commission. We'll discuss the best strategy for the buyer's agent commission when we create your listing plan.

We charge a simple, transparent flat listing fee that is agreed upon upfront. This fee is only paid at closing, from the proceeds of your home sale, just like a traditional commission. There are no hidden charges or upfront costs to get started. If your home doesn't sell, you don't pay our listing fee.
